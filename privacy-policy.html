Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 8, 2025

Summary: We collect minimal data (username, email, game progress) to provide puzzle services. We never sell your data. You can delete your account anytime.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Username: Your chosen username
  • Email Address: For account recovery and important notifications
  • Password: Stored as a secure hash (we cannot see your actual password)
  • Display Name: How you appear to other users
  • Avatar: Optional profile picture

1.2 Game Data

To provide puzzle services and track your progress, we collect:

  • Puzzle completion times
  • Scores and rankings
  • Game history and statistics
  • Achievement unlocks
  • Battle Pass progress (if subscribed)
  • In-game preferences (themes, settings)

1.3 Payment Information

If you subscribe to Premium:

  • Payment processing: Handled entirely by Stripe (we never see your credit card number)
  • What we store: Stripe customer ID, subscription status, subscription tier
  • Your rights: You can cancel anytime, and we'll delete payment references

1.4 Automatically Collected Information

  • IP Address: For security and rate limiting
  • Browser Type: To optimize the experience
  • Device Information: For responsive design
  • Usage Analytics: Via PostHog (anonymized)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data solely to:

  • ✅ Provide puzzle services and save your progress
  • ✅ Display leaderboards and rankings
  • ✅ Process Premium subscriptions
  • ✅ Send important account notifications (password resets, subscription renewals)
  • ✅ Prevent fraud and abuse
  • ✅ Improve the service through analytics

We NEVER:

  • ❌ Sell your data to third parties
  • ❌ Send marketing emails (unless you opt-in)
  • ❌ Share your data with advertisers
  • ❌ Track you across other websites

3. Data Sharing and Third Parties

3.1 Service Providers

We share data with trusted services to operate The London Sudoku:

  • Vercel: Hosting and infrastructure
  • Neon: Database hosting
  • Stripe: Payment processing (see their privacy policy)
  • PostHog: Analytics (anonymized)
  • Clerk: Authentication services

3.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, such as:

  • Responding to court orders or legal processes
  • Protecting our rights and property
  • Preventing fraud or abuse
  • Ensuring user safety

3.3 Business Transfers

If The London Sudoku is acquired or merged, your data may be transferred to the new owner. You'll be notified via email 30 days in advance.

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Where We Store Data

  • Location: United States (via Vercel and Neon)
  • Encryption: All data encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest
  • Backups: Automatic daily backups retained for 30 days

4.2 Security Measures

  • ✅ Password hashing with bcrypt (industry standard)
  • ✅ Rate limiting to prevent brute force attacks
  • ✅ CSRF protection on all state-changing operations
  • ✅ SQL injection prevention via parameterized queries
  • ✅ Regular security audits

4.3 Data Retention

  • Active accounts: Data retained indefinitely while account is active
  • Deleted accounts: Data permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Inactive accounts: Accounts inactive for 3+ years may be deleted (you'll be notified via email)
  • Legal holds: Data may be retained longer if required by law

5. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

You have the right to:

5.1 Access Your Data

Request a copy of all data we have about you. Email privacy@thelondonsudoku.com and we'll provide a JSON file within 30 days.

5.2 Correct Your Data

Update your profile information anytime in Account Settings.

5.3 Delete Your Data

Delete your account anytime:

  • Go to Account Settings → Delete Account
  • All personal data deleted within 30 days
  • Leaderboard entries anonymized (shown as "Deleted User")
  • Subscription automatically canceled

5.4 Object to Processing

You can opt-out of:

  • Analytics tracking (via Do Not Track browser setting)
  • Email notifications (Account Settings)
  • Public leaderboard display (Account Settings → Hide from Leaderboards)

5.5 Data Portability

Export your game data in JSON format anytime (Account Settings → Export Data).

6. Cookies and Tracking

6.1 Essential Cookies

Required for the service to work:

  • session_token: Keeps you logged in (expires after 30 days)
  • csrf_token: Security protection (session only)

6.2 Analytics Cookies

PostHog analytics (anonymized):

  • Can be disabled via Do Not Track browser setting
  • No personally identifiable information collected

6.3 Preference Cookies

  • theme: Your color scheme preference
  • settings: Game preferences (hints, notes, etc.)

7. Children's Privacy

Age Requirement: You must be 13+ years old to use The London Sudoku.

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has created an account, contact us immediately at privacy@thelondonsudoku.com and we'll delete it within 48 hours.

8. International Users

For EU/UK users: We comply with GDPR. Data transfers are protected by:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses with service providers
